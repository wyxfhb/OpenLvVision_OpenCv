The function cv::eigen calculates eigenvalues only, or both eigenvalues and eigenvectors of a symmetric matrix src:

{{< katex >}}$ \texttt{src} \cdot \texttt{eigenvectors.row}(i)^T = \texttt{eigenvalues.at}(i) \cdot \texttt{eigenvectors.row}(i)^T ${{< /katex >}}
{{< alert context="warning" text="Use eigenNonSymmetric for calculation of real eigenvalues and eigenvectors of non-symmetric matrix." />}}

For detailed information, please refer to the OpenCV documentation
