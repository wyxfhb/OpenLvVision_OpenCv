The function cv::dft performs one of the following:

  • Inverse Fourier transform of a 1D vector of N elements:
    {{< katex >}}$ \begin{aligned} X' &= (F^{(N)})^{-1} \cdot Y = (F^{(N)})^* \cdot Y \\ X &= \frac{1}{N} \cdot X' \end{aligned} ${{< /katex >}}

    where

    {{< katex >}}$ F^{(N)*} = \left( \mathrm{Re}(F^{(N)}) - \mathrm{Im}(F^{(N)}) \right)^{T} ${{< /katex >}}
  • Inverse Fourier transform of an $M \times N$ matrix:
    {{< katex >}}$ \begin{aligned} X' &= (F^{(M)})^* \cdot Y \cdot (F^{(N)})^* \\ X &= \frac{1}{M \cdot N} \cdot X' \end{aligned} ${{< /katex >}}
